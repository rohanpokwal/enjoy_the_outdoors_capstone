Our Mission

"Discover. Explore. Preserve"

At Wilderness Locator, we are dedicated to connecting people with the natural beauty of the United States by providing an easy-to-use platform for locating national parks and mountains. Our mission is to inspire outdoor exploration and conservation, fostering a deep appreciation for our country's natural heritage. We also strive to raise funds and awareness to support the creation of new parks, ensuring future generations have the opportunity to enjoy and protect these treasured landscapes.
